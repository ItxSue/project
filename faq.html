Frequently Asked Questions

Have any nail related questions? Check below:

Apply a nail strengthening base coat regularly, avoid harsh chemicals, and include biotin-rich foods in your diet.

Trim your nails every 1-2 weeks to maintain a neat shape and prevent breakage.

Soak your nails in acetone and gently scrape the polish off with a cuticle stick to avoid damaging your nails.

Splitting nails are often caused by dryness or overexposure to water. Use a good hand and nail moisturizer and avoid harsh nail tools.

Always use a base coat before applying polish and give your nails a break from polish every few weeks.
