How to Make Your Nails Look Well-Manicured at Home

  1. Push back your cuticles gently using a cuticle pusher or orange stick.
  2. File nails to your desired shape (square, oval, almond, etc.).
  3. Buff nails lightly to create a smooth surface.
  4. Apply a strengthening base coat to protect and nourish your nails.
  5. Finish with a clear top coat for a glossy, professional look.

With just a few simple steps, you can keep your nails looking salon-fresh without ever leaving home. Let us help you elevate your nail care game!
