How to Make Your Nails Look Well-Manicured

  • Push back your cuticles gently to keep your nails neat.
  • File your nails to a shape that complements your hands.
  • Buff your nail plate for a smooth and shiny finish.
  • Apply a strengthening base coat to protect and fortify your nails.
  • Finish with a clear top coat for that extra polished look.
